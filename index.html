Pro Scrabble Tips

Strategies used by tournament Scrabble players to dominate every game.

01

Play the S wisely

An S tile is worth holding — it can pluralize any noun and often opens 2-word plays for massive bonus points.

02

Target Triple Word Scores

A 7-letter word hitting a TWS corner scores 50+ bonus points. Always scan for open TWS squares first.

03

Know your 2-letter words

QI, ZA, XI, AX — knowing all 101 valid 2-letter Scrabble words dramatically increases your options every turn.

04

Dump high-value duplicates

Two V's or two U's? Get rid of them. Duplicate tiles of the same high-value letter reduce your flexibility drastically.

05

Parallel plays double points

Placing tiles parallel to existing words creates multiple small words simultaneously, often doubling your total score.

06

Use -ING, -ED, -ER freely

Common suffixes dramatically expand what you can build. Keep a vowel-consonant balance to hit them consistently.

What dictionary does ScrambleSnap use? +
Our primary dictionary combines TWL (Tournament Word List used in North American Scrabble), OSPD5 (Official Scrabble Players Dictionary), and SOWPODS (used in international Scrabble). This 100,000+ word database is updated regularly to include new official additions.
Can I use blank tiles / wildcards? +
Yes — Pro users can use ? as a wildcard (blank tile) in the input. For example, entering "AP?LE" will find words where the ? can be any letter. This is essential for Scrabble where blank tiles count as 0 points but can represent any letter.
Does it work on mobile and offline? +
Absolutely. ScrambleSnap is a Progressive Web App (PWA) — you can install it on iOS and Android from your browser. The core solver dictionary is cached locally, so it works fully offline. Perfect for playing Scrabble on planes or anywhere without internet.

ScrambleSnap is faster (client-side, no server), shows definitions inline, supports all major game dictionaries simultaneously, works offline as a PWA, and offers a significantly cleaner interface without intrusive ads. Scrabble Cheat Sheet

Quick reference for serious Scrabble players.

Q

Q without U words

QI (11pts), QOPH (18pts), QANAT (14pts), QIGONG (16pts). Memorize these — they're game-changers when you draw Q without U.

Z

High Z words

ZAX (19pts), ZEP (14pts), ZOEAE (16pts), ZOEÆ. Z words near bonus squares can win games single-handedly.

7

7-letter bingos

Playing all 7 tiles earns a 50-point bonus. Common bingo-friendly racks include SATINE, RETINA, SATIRE. Our solver highlights potential bingos automatically.
